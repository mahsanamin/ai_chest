# Document Formatting Rules

## Separators
- Never use `---` (horizontal rules) in any document.

## Content
- No repetition of the same information across different sections.
- Keep information to the point. No filler or verbose explanations.
- Preserve exact phrasing from key speakers (e.g., the meeting lead / decision-maker). Use their actual words, not paraphrased versions.

## Structure
- Use headers and tables for organization, not decorative elements.
